Radicals

Radicals Name Mnemonic
亅 Barb This radical looks like a barb. It resembles a piece of barbed wire.
大 Big Imagine this radical as a guy doing a T pose. His arms can go really far and his legs are very big.
工 Construction Think of it as a sideways construction bar, a big steel bar they use to make the frame for a building during construction. Like how you are using radicals to build kanjis!
十 Cross Don't know how you can forget this. It's literally a cross.
丶 Drop This radical is like a drop of liquid, can you see it?
入 Enter Think of it as a teepee, like the Native Americans used. Think of entering the teepee.
ハ Fins With fins think about a fish clapping his fins as they were hands.
一 Ground This is a straight line, you know what else is (usually) a straight line? The ground.
亠 Lid It looks like a lid to a pot, or a pan on the stove.
山 Mountain Connect the top part of the vertical lines and it'll look like a mountain!
口 Mouth Think of it as a big mouth of someone really surprised on what you just said.
九 Nine You can see the letter n and i in this radical. The curved stroke on the right represents the i and everything is n. N and i make Ni which is the first two letters of nine.
人 Person It's a the big radical without arms! It still represents a person. Also if you are an Attack on Titan fan, this radical/kanji is in the name! 進撃の巨人.
力 Power Think of it as a guy using flexing off his MUSCULAR arm. so MUCH POWER!!!!!!
勹 Prison This radical sort of represents a prison because the right side wraps over and locks in a prisoner.
川 River It looks like a river from above!
七 Seven You can actually see a seven in this radical! Look at the bottom left part of the radical and you can see an angle that represents seven.
丿 Slide It resembles a slide but from a side view. Remember those slides at recess in primary school?
日 Sun There are actually no circles in Japanese script! So they use squares instead. As you can see if you make this circular its a sphere with a line through it which represents the sun! The middle line could be a cloud or whatever! Use your imagination.
ト Toe This radical looks the same as the katakana character ト (to), which sounds like the word toe.
木 Tree Do you see the tree in this radical? The middle line is the trunk and the other 4 lines are the leaves and branches!
二 Two This radical is easy, just count the amount of lines! There are two lines and the radical name is two!
女 Woman This radical is 2 Xs stacked up on top of each other. Now if you study in school you should know that women have 2 XXs for chromosomes. If you are doing well in school you should be able to do well learning this radical too!

Kanji

Kanji Name On'yomi Kun'yomi Nanori Mnemonic
上 Above じょう うえ, あ, のぼ, うわ, かみ None This kanji is made up of the ト toe radical and the 一 ground radical. You are stumped because you want to know why there is a toe above ground. When you think about it more, the thumb probably belongs to the weird old farmer, Jourm (じょう).
下 Below か, げ した, さ, くだ, お None There is a toe below ground, you know there is a toe in the ground so you start digging for it. After digging for a while you hit something. You dig it out and its a car(か)!
大 Big たい, だい おお ひろ The big radical and the big kanji are the exact same, if you know your radicals you know this kanji. Imagine there is the big guy representing this radical/kanji. He/she is wearing a very big Tye Dye(たい, だい) shirt, everything about this big person is huge, even the shirt.
工 Construction こう, く None None The construction radical and the construction kanji are the same, see you've got to know your radicals man. The kanji also means industry, which is a synonym of construction. Now Wanikani's mnemonic includes Kouichi which is one of the creators of the website, so for this example we are going to use Kouichi Hirose from Jojo: Diamond is Unbreakable. Just remember how short he is and how unfitting it would be for him as a construction job. こういち.
八 Eight はち や, よう None This is a slightly modified ハ Fins radical! Do you see the fins radical? Imagine you go fishing and you get 4 fishes with 2 fins each. When you count all the fins you get 8! You open a large hatch(はち) and dump the fish in there. Eight fins at a time or else the boat will sink because too much is weighted at the same time!
入 Enter にゅう はい, い None The Enter radical and Enter kanji are the same, make sure you learn your radicals yo! You enter a teepee and you get a surprise present! A new(にゅう) teepee! A neeeeeeeeeeew teepee.
山 Mountain さん やま None The mountain radical and kanji are the same once again! Now let's dive a little into honorifics, you call people their name and -san at the end if you want to be respectable! So mountains talking to each other woud be something like "Hello Everest-san." -san (さん)
口 Mouth こう, く くち None Again the radical Just imagine Kouichi-kun(こういち) saying something to you like a brick is about to fall on your head so he's telling you to get out the way with his cool mouth.
九 Nine く, きゅう ここの None When you were nine your favorite foods were cookies (く) and cucumbers (きゅう). Yum yum!
一 One いち, いつ ひと かず This kanji is made up of the ground radical. A way to remember this kanji is that how many grounds are there? There is only one ground and it is that ground. For reading just think about laying on the grass outside completely naked, after a while you start to feel itchy(いち)
人 Person にん, じん ひと, と None
